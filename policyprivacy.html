--- layout: default title: Privacy Policy permalink: /privacypolicy.html --- # Privacy Policy **Celebrating Social** (a.k.a. “the App”, “we”, “us”) **Effective date:** 11 November 2025 ## 1. Introduction Celebrating Social is a mobile-first social-commerce platform that lets users post multimedia content, interact (like, comment, repost, gift), sell products & services, accept bookings, and withdraw earnings. We respect your privacy and comply with Google Play Developer Policy, GDPR, CCPA, and applicable national laws. ## 2. Data We Collect ### A. Information You Provide - **Account data:** name, username, email, phone, password, profile photo, bio, date of birth. - **Content:** posts, stories, comments, messages, live-streams, product listings, calendar slots. - **Payment data:** payout method (bank, PayPal, crypto address), transaction IDs, tax info (if required). - **Support tickets:** any info you choose to include. ### B. Automatically Collected Data - **Device IDs** (Android ID, Ad-ID), OS version, language, time-zone, IP address. - **Log data:** crash traces, performance metrics, feature usage. - **Precise location** (only if you grant runtime permission). - **Cookies / local storage** for session management and analytics. ### C. Third-Party Sources - **Login providers** (Google, Facebook, Apple, Twitter) – name, email, avatar. - **Payment processors** (Stripe, PayPal, Coinbase) – confirmation tokens only. - **Shipping carriers** – tracking numbers you voluntarily add. ## 3. Why We Process Data - Provide core functionality (posting, feed, messaging, checkout). - Facilitate payments & withdrawals. - Detect fraud, spam, and policy violations. - Personalise content and ads. - Send service notifications, security alerts, and optional marketing (opt-out anytime). - Comply with legal obligations (tax, accounting, law-enforcement requests). ## 4. Legal Bases (EEA Users) - **Contract:** account creation & service delivery. - **Legitimate interests:** security, analytics, product improvement. - **Consent:** push notifications, marketing emails, location sharing. - **Legal obligation:** financial record-keeping. ## 5. Sharing & Disclosure We **never sell** personal data. We only share: - **With other users** – whatever you post publicly or sell. - **Service providers** – cloud hosting (AWS, GCP), payments (Stripe, PayPal), analytics (Firebase), crash reporting (Sentry). - **Authorities** – when legally compelled or to protect safety. - **Business transfer** – in a merger or acquisition, subject to this policy. ## 6. Data Retention - **Posts / messages:** until you delete them or your account. - **Transaction records:** 7 years (tax law). - **Device logs:** 90 days. - **Marketing opt-out lists:** perpetual suppression. ## 7. Your Rights - **Access, rectify, delete** your data inside Settings → Privacy or by emailing privacy@celebrating.app. - **Export** your content (JSON/ZIP) in Settings → Download My Data. - **Withdraw consent** at any time; functionality may be limited. - **EEA users:** lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. ## 8. Security - TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest. - PCI-DSS compliant tokenisation for card data. - 2-factor authentication available. - Regular penetration tests and bug-bounty program. ## 9. Children The App is **not directed to children under 13** (or under 16 in the EEA). We delete accounts reported as under-age unless parental consent is verified. ## 10. Changes We will notify you via in-app alert or email at least 30 days before material changes. Continued use constitutes acceptance. ## 11.
